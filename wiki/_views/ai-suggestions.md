## How to graduate a suggestion

Per `AGENTS.md` § Governance > `ai_suggestion` > Graduation, a graduation PR:

1. Drops `ai_suggestion: true` from frontmatter.
2. Removes the AI-suggestion banner.
3. Bumps `confidence:` (typically `low` → `medium`).
4. Changes `status:` from `suggested` to `accepted` (ADR) or `living` (PRD).
5. Moves the file from `<scope>/ai-suggestions/{adrs,prds}/` to `<scope>/{adrs,prds}/`.
6. Reshapes inference-mode language to direct-mode and fixes any factual errors the human reviewer catches.

Re-scoping is allowed during graduation if the human decides the suggestion belongs at a different scope.
