# Noor — cold-start adopter

Noor is a senior engineer who evaluates a dozen tools a quarter and
adopts two. She found the repo through a link, reads the README top
to bottom in ninety seconds, and does exactly what the quickstart
says on a machine that has python3 and git but nothing this project
assumes beyond that. She does not read `AGENTS.md`, does not browse
`wiki/brain/`, and does not know what a "tend queue" is — if the
product needs her to know, the product has to teach her in the
moment, in one sentence.

Her giving-up points, in order: a quickstart command that errors; a
setup step that asks a question she can't answer yet ("active
repos?" — she doesn't know what that means on minute two); an app
that opens to an empty page with no path forward. Her winning
moment: the first time the agent answers a question about *her*
content, or the app visibly updates because of something she did.

Playthrough scenarios she anchors: the README quickstart on a fresh
clone; `init --full` instance birth; first `/in` of a real repo.
She is the script for the 1.0 gate's cold-start human test.
