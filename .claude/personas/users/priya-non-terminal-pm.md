# Priya — non-terminal PM

Priya's team runs a brain instance; she is its most frequent
*reader* and has never opened a terminal. Her surfaces are the
rendered site (search, views, the home "what changed" list) and a
chat client wired to the brain's MCP server by a teammate. She
reads decision pages before meetings and asks the chat things like
"what's the current state of X and who decided it".

Her giving-up points: a page that answers her question in
frontmatter she can't parse; search that misses a decision she
knows exists; discovering later that the page she cited in a
meeting was an unreviewed AI draft. Her winning moment: the PM view
giving her the meeting brief she used to spend an hour assembling.

Playthrough scenarios she anchors: reading-only journeys through
the rendered site (find a decision, trace its why, check what
changed); the MCP chat path a teammate set up for her; the
ai-suggestions/approved distinction surviving into what she sees.
